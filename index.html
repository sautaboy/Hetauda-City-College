About Our College

Hetauda City College is affiliated with Tribhuvan University and is offering B.Sc CSIT program. In 2075 BS, Hetauda City College also started offering BCA program.

Hetauda City College is located in Kantirajpath-4, Hetauda, and was founded in 2013 AD (2070 BS). It has been providing high-quality information technology and management education for many years. It is known as Hetauda's first IT college and offers superior IT and Management education from knowledgeable academics.
